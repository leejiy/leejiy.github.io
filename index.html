Hello, I am a Ph.D. student in School of Computing at KAIST. I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Insik Shin and enjoying research with talented people in the Cyber-Physical Systems Lab. My current research interests are general topics in security and privacy, specifically web vulnerability research, side-channel attacks, and static/dynamic program analysis. I'm also interested in system design and analysis with timing guarantees in Cyber-Physical Systems.
