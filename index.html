Assistant Professor
School of Computer Science and Engineering,
Kyungpook National University
jiyeon at knu dot ac dot kr

I am an assistant professor in School of Computer Science and Engineering at Kyungpook National University. Prior to KNU, I completed Ph.D. in School of Computing at KAIST. My research interests are general topics on security and privacy, specifically web vulnerability research and cyber-physical systems security.
